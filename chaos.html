TRAIL STOP #5

Study Series

By Yuko Otomo

Yuko Otomo is a visual artist & a bilingual poet/writer of Japanese origin. Her latest books are STUDY & Other Poems on Art (by Ugly Duckling Presse) & Elements (by the Feral Press). She is currently a contributing writer arteidolia.com where she posts her critical writing mostly on visual art.

Study 5: space & boundary

In space
But there is

A boundary

In the space
You face

Four corners
Are not the end
But the beginning
Of something bigger

Even deeper

So stop facing
The space

Instead

Become it
